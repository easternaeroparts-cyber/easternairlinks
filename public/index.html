About Us

Connecting Nepal,
One Flight at a Time

Eastern AirLinks is a regional carrier headquartered at Gautam Buddha International Airport in Bhairahawa. Our mission is to make air travel accessible, reliable, and affordable for communities across Nepal.

We believe every Nepali deserves fast, safe connectivity between cities. Whether you're traveling for business, family, or adventure, Eastern AirLinks is your trusted partner in the skies.
